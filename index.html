About Catify

A startup obsession with the small stuff

Catify started in a tiny kitchen with a picky rescue and a spreadsheet. Today we’re a distributed team of pet people, food scientists, and designers united by one rule: if we wouldn’t feed it to our own cats, it doesn’t ship.

We’re playful in our brand and serious in our standards—because trust isn’t a vibe, it’s a stack of decisions you repeat every single batch.
